I need an extra feature in this app. How can I request it?

You can contact us to request a customization in the app.

Is this app compatible with Odoo Enterprise as well as Community?

Yes, the app works perfectly well with Odoo Enterprise (On-premise and Odoo.SH) as well as Community. Odoo Online (Cloud) does not allow installation of third-party apps and hence this app cannot be installed on Odoo Online.

Do I get free upgrades and support if I purchase the app?

If you purchase our app for Odoo Version 12 and prior, we will provide you access to all the versions up to 12 depending on their availability. Starting version 13, every version of the app will be sold separately. We offer free support for a duration of 90 days from the date of purchase. Kindly read our support policy to know the details.

Can I export the products from Odoo to ShipStation?

No, you cannot export the products from Odoo to ShipStation.

Can I get the Shipping Labels and Tracking Link from the shipping providers in Odoo?

Yes, with this connector you can get the Shipping Labels and Tracking Link from ShipStation to Odoo directly.

Is it necessary to synchronize the products from ShipStation to Odoo before using shipping services?

Yes, you need to sync the products from ShipStation to Odoo before to make the shipping services work seamlessly. If you do not synchronize the products, you cannot Export Orders from Odoo to ShipStation.

Can I modify the delivery address once the label is generated?

Once your label is generated, you cannot modify the delivery address of the recipient. In this case, you need to cancel the whole Sales Order and create the new one.
